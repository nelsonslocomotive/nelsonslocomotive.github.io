4 Jul 11 Walter Bercaw tells me that he has sold a number of Heisler casting sets and I'm now beginning to get questions and folks are finding errors and inconsistencies in the drawings.  

Everyone: please let me know of any errors or inconsistencies you find.

One set of inconsistencies involved the exhaust passages in the valve part of the cylinders and the mating recesses in the exhaust manifolds.  The Heisler Engine Design III and Heisler Engine Design IV pages were updated to show corrections.
