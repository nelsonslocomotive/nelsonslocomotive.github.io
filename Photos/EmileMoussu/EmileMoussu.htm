Emile Mousse sent the following photos of the shay he build using Ken Schroeder's castings.  Emile is located in the northern part of Brittany, France.   (Brittany is in the most western province of France, jutting out into the Atlantic directly south of England.  It is south and west of the Normandy landing beaches and includes the cities of Rennes and Brest. )    The dinning car is of particular interest.  Also note that some of the track is dual gauge. Emile says that many of the French enthusiasts find the Shay strange.
